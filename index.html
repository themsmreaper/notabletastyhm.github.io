Tasty Rotten

Notable Tasty Henchmen

The Royal Family and Royal Guards

The royal candy family is a group of candy people who rule the candylands. There is Kingdy the King, Princdy the Prince and the King's Heir. There are also various guards, some with spears, some with shields and some that are armored.

Butter Fingers

Butter Fingers is a sentient stick of butter that came to life in the kitchen of a chef named Mixer Mackey. No one really knows how he came to life, not even Mackey...

Mixer Mackey

Mixer Mackey! The Mixer! A chef who has his own restaurant in the Big City where the main gimmick is that everything he cooks requires mixing, how fun :)

Egg Guy

Egg Guy is a self-proclaimed "evil genius" who pesters the citizens of the Big City (especially the Don's henchmen). Like Butter Fingers, he was brought to life in Mixer Mackey's restaurant... How does he keep doing that?

Pan Jake

Pan Jake is another guard in the Candylands. He guards a side of the outer walls.

Pizzamind

Pizzamind is a sort of pizza monk wizard guy
